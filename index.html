About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is a fast-paced roguelike survival game built in Unity and inspired by classics like Vampire Survivors and Survivor.io. You control a hero dropped into a map swarming with enemies. Your job is simple: stay alive, grow stronger, and outlast every wave the game throws at you.

Each run feels different. Enemies spawn in growing numbers, experience gems drop as you defeat them, and every level-up lets you pick new weapons, passive buffs, or evolved abilities. Bosses appear at set intervals with unique attack patterns, forcing you to adapt your build on the fly.

Whether you have five minutes on your phone or a longer session on desktop, Monster Survivors delivers quick, satisfying runs with deep build variety — all playable instantly at willmaster.online with no installation.

How to Play

Desktop Controls

  • W A S D or Arrow Keys — Move
  • Mouse — Aim direction
  • Left Click — Attack / Confirm
  • Space — Pause
  • Esc — Back / Menu
  • E or Enter — Select

Mobile Controls

  • Virtual Joystick — Move your character
  • On-screen Buttons — Menu and actions
  • Swipe — Navigate menus and selections
  • Tap — Confirm choices and interact

Tip: Focus on area-of-effect weapons early, pick complementary passives as you level, and keep moving — standing still is the fastest way to lose a run.
